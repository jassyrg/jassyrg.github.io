Premium Upgrades are available to purchase for Kreds, Kongregate's premium currency, that can be purchased for real money in the "Shop" tab. Some of these give permanent bonuses that persist as long as you are logged into your Kongregate account, while others are one-time boosts.

Permanent Upgrades

These upgrades persist through Abdications, Reincarnations, and even hard resets They are linked to your Kongregate account and you must be logged in for them to work.

The Gift bonuses can be turned off in the options (which can be necessary to unlock some Secret Trophies).

Note: All bonuses are multiplicative with each other.

Note: All gifts are multiplicative.

Note: All 3 gifts = 8.41x prod bonus.

Note: All 3 gifts = 10% mana regen.

Note: All 3 gifts = x1.26 FC chance.

Note: All 3 gifts = x42.875 offline bonus.

Note: All 3 gifts = +0.5 addive mana regen.

Note: All 3 gifts = +2 clicks per sec.

Note: Each gift gives 250% offline production.

Event Sale

During certain Events you will be able to buy "Gift of the Heroes" at 50% off

Gift of the Heroes

Cost: 50 Kreds

Effects: Increase the production of all buildings by 25%.

Effects: Increase clicking reward by 15%.

Effects: Increase Faction Coin chance additively and multiplicatively by 5%.

Effects: Increase offline gains by 250%.

Effects: Provides 0.50 mana per second.

Effects: Grants +25,000 gold and +20 faction coins of each unlocked faction after every abdication or hard reset. (in other words, you can immediately join the Vanilla Faction of your choice after abdicating.)

Effects: Buying this upgrade unlocks a better upgrade.

Gift of the Kings

Cost: 100 Kreds

Effects: Increases the production of all buildings by 50%.

Effects: Increase clicking reward by 30%.

Effects: Increase Faction Coin chance additively and multiplicatively by 5%.

Effects: Increase offline gains by 250%.

Effects: Provides a special assistant which actually autoclicks twice a second (triggering all real click effects and increasing the click stats) and counts as 2 common assistants while offline.

Effects: Grants 10 Qa (1e16) coins and 500 Faction coins for each faction upon any reset.

Effects: Buying this upgrade will unlock a more powerful upgrade.

Gift of the Gods

Cost: 200 Kreds

Effects: Increase the production of all buildings by 100%.

Effects: Increase clicking reward by 50%.

Effects: Increase Faction Coin chance additively and multiplicatively by 15%.

Effects: Increase offline gains by 250%.

Effects: Increase mana regeneration by 10%.

Effects: Grants 100 Td (1e44) coins and 25000 Faction Coins for each unlocked faction upon any reset.

Ruby Power

Cost: 1 Coin

Unlock Requirements: Find 1 Ruby

Effect: Unlocks the power of Rubies, increasing the production of all buildings by 100% and allowing you to purchase upgrades which increases all aspects of your production.

Ruby Power starts at 100% and increases based on the amount of rubies you have.

Rubies are not affected by soft-resets.
