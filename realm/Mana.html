Automatic Casting

Requires: 60,000 mana produced

Upgrade Cost: 100 B (1E11), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Allows autocasting of spells by CTRL+Clicking them.

Upgrade Effect: While offline, spells are not cast, instead increases mana produced amount by 10% of your mana regen per second.(need at least 1 spell in autocasting mode)

Improved Autocasting

Requirement: 2M (2E6) mana produced

Upgrade Cost: 200 Qi (2E20), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Increases mana regeneration by 5%.

Upgrade Effect: While offline, increases mana produced amount by an additional 15% of your mana regeneration per second.(need at least 1 spell in autocasting mode)

Masterful Autocasting

Requirement: 6M (6E6) mana produced

Upgrade Cost: 300 Oc (3E29), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Increases mana regeneration by 10%.

Upgrade Effect: While offline, increases mana produced amount by an additional 35% of your mana regeneration per second.

Priority Autocasting

Requirement: 12M (1.2E7) mana produced

Upgrade Cost: 400 Ud (4E38), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Allows you to prioritize the order of spell casting.

Note: Spells with lower priority will be cast only if the higher priority spells are already active.

Upgrade Effect: While offline, improves spell cast amount by 1 per minute.

Contingency Autocasting

Requirement: 20M (2E7) mana produced

Upgrade Cost: 500 Qad (5E47), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Allows you to set a minimum amount of mana before starting to Autocast.

Note: A slide bar will appear when you CTRL+Click on the mana bar.

Note: Contingency-slidebar By adjusting its height you will set a minimum amount of mana below which spells won't be cast.

Note: Once your mana reaches the threshold, spells will follow the priority rules.

Note: The contingency loop will break when you can't cast a spell, at which point the cycle will reset.

Upgrade Effect: While offline, improves spell cast amount by an additional 2 per minute.

Planned Autocasting

Requirement: 30M (3E7) mana produced

Upgrade Cost: 600 Spd (6E56), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Allows you to set Primary and Secondary spells.

Note: Primary spells (Set with CTRL+Click) will fire when you have max mana with Contingency Autocasting.

Note: Secondary spells (Set with SHIFT+Click) will fire as soon as you have the mana, as long as the primary spells are ALL still active.

Upgrade Effect: While offline, increases mana produced by an additional 10% of your mana regeneration per second and your spells cast amount by an additional 2 per minute.

Efficient Autocasting

Requirement: 50M (5E7) mana produced

Upgrade Cost: 700 Vg (7E65), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Allows you to select Independent Spells

Note: A bronze icon (Set with CTRL+SHIFT+Click) to work outside of contingency settings. These spells disregard your contingency rules and are casted as soon as you have enough mana for them.

Upgrade Effect: While offline, increases mana produced amount by an additional 10% of your mana regen per second and spell cast amount by 2 per minute.

Reverse Autocasting

Requirements: Reach 1.5e8 mana and unlock the Reverse Autocasting upgrade. Can only be unlocked after Ascension 1 at Reincarnation 40+.

Effect: Allows you to manually reset spells with a 2 second cooldown per spell.

Note: Can not reset spells with a fixed duration.

Note: Only while offline, increase your mana produced amount by an additional 10% of your Mana Regeneration per second, and your spells cast amount multiplicatively based on your offline Mana Regeneration.

Formula: (1000 * log10(1 + x)), where x is Mana Regeneration

Cost: 3.2 Dc (3.2e33), A2+: Free

Tiered Autocasting

Requirement: 200M (2E8) mana produced (Total this R), R42+

Note: This trophy unlocks at R40, The upgrade unlocks at R42

Upgrade Cost: 100 Qid (1.0e50), A2+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Allows you to set the maximum tier you wish to autocast each spell to.

Note: In the spell tooltip, use the diamond icon to change its mode until you get to the desired maximum tier number. Spells will still follow their previous priority rules.

Upgrade Effect: Only while offline, increase mana produced by an additional 10% (for a total of 90%) of your mana regeneration per second and your spells cast amount and your spells cast amount multiplicatively based on your offline mana regeneration.

Formula: (1000 * log10(1 + x))

Arcane Brilliance: Formula and Effect are the same for all.

Passive effect: Reduces the time requirement for unlocking new spell tiers.

Formula: 43200*((T-0.5*A)^2-T+0.5*A))*0.98^(R-T+0.5*A-42), where T is tier, A is amount of arcane blillance trophies, R is reincarnation.

Coin Pillage

Requirement: Cast the Tax Collection spell 30 times in less than 5 seconds.

Upgrade Cost: 1 Dc (1E33), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Tax Collection also finds 30 seconds worth of Faction Coins.

True Night

Requirement: Activate Undead's Night Time spell after 11 pm and before 6 am.(This Time is YOUR local time)

Upgrade Cost: 1 M (1E6), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Increase Night Time boost to assistants production to 40% per Necropolis.

Lightning Storm

Requirement: Cast Titan's Lightning Strike 100 times in a single game.

Upgrade Cost: 10 Dd (1E40), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Increase Lightning Strike multiplier to 500% per Iron Stronghold

Halls of Balance

Requirement: Make Druid's Grand Balance affect Hall of Legends.

Upgrade Cost: 10 Dd (1E40), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Increase Grand Balance duration by 4 seconds and lower its cost by 200 mana

Tip: Buy at least 1 of everything and buy 0 Hall of Legends.

Faceless Overmind

Requirement: Affiliate with the Faceless 5 times in a row. (Buy Faceless Trade Treaty 5x in a row)

Upgrade Cost: 10 Dd (1E40), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Increase Brainwave tick to 3% per second

Minedwarf

Requirement: Playing as Dwarf, gain 30,000 Faction Coins using the Dwarf spell Diamond Pickaxe in a single game.

Upgrade Cost: 100 Sxd (1.53), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Increases Diamond Pickaxe bonus to "Faction Coin Find Chance" to 300%.

Perfect Combo

Requirement: Cast the Drow spell Combo Strike, 100 times in a row (not casting any other spell)

Upgrade Cost: 100 Sxd (1.53), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Increases Combo Strike bonus.

Formula: (40 * x ^ 0.9), where x is the number of times you have cast Combo Strike.

Sun Blessing

Requirement: Activate the Elf spell Moon Blessing between 6am and 1 pm.

Upgrade Cost: 1 M (1E6), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: The treasure is autoclicked 15 times per second while Moon Blessing is active.

Fairy Choir

Requirement: Cast the Fairy spell Fairy Chanting while having 45 or more assistants.

Upgrade Cost: 1 M (1E6), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Fairy Chanting triples your assistants for its duration.

God's Rest

Requirement: Cast the Angel spell God's Hand on a Sunday.

Upgrade Cost: 1 M (1E6), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: While this spell is active, you will find random Faction Coins based on your mana regen each time you cast an other spell.

Greed Drive

Requirement: Cast the Goblin spell Goblin's Greed 100 times in a row in a single game. The chain is interrupted by casting different spells.

Note: The Goblin's Central Bank upgrade (For each TC cast, you get a free TC) does not count, even though it is counted as an extra spell-cast in the stats.)

Upgrade Cost: 1 M (1E6), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Goblin's Greed cost 150 less mana.

Hell Rush

Requirement: Cast the Demon spell Hellfire Blast in the first 60 seconds of a playtime.

Upgrade Cost: 1 M (1E6), A1+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Hellfire Blast also boosts Halls of Legends.

Dragon's Roar

Requirement: Cast Dragon's Breath while having at least 4 other active spells.

Upgrade Cost: 1 Qad (1.0e45), A2+ Free

Upgrade Effect: Dragon's Breath multiplicatively increases Faction Coin find chance based on its duration.

Formula: (6 * x ^0.6), where x is duration in seconds

Chrono Loading

Requirements: R125+, Cast Precognition with at least 100 Qa (1e17) mana regeneration.

Cost: 10 Octg (1e118)

Effect: A fraction of Precognition duration is added to time spent in this game.

Effect: Scale off invisible spell duration modifiers.

Formula: (25 * x ^ 0.55), where x is Precognition duration.(seconds)

Note: Precognition raises regen itself. Make sure that you have 100 Qa (1e17) with it not active.

Persistent Entropy

Requirements: R125+, Cast Limited Wish 100 times within 30 minutes of a new game.

Cost: 10 Octg (1e118)

Effect: Also adds 150 casts to Limited wish formula.

Reality Crater

Requirements: R125+, Cast Infinite Spiral with at least 10 excavation resets this game.

Cost: 10 Octg (1e118)

Effect: Infinite Spiral also reduces excavation cost multiplier based on assistants owned.

Formula: (0.001 * log(1 + x)), where x is assistants owned.
